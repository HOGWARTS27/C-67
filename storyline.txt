story*
once upon a time in a forest, there lived a monkey who resided on a berrytree which was on a bank of a river.
In the same forest, there lived a crocodile and its wife. One day, the crocodile came to the bank of the river and 
monkey gave him some berries and they became good friends.
 The crocodile came the next day for more fruits as he loved them. Monkey gave him some fruits for his wife also.
